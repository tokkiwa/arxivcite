Welcome to arxivcite

arxivcite is a simple tool to generate citations for arXiv papers in various formats.

How to Use

Change "arxiv.org/pdf/..." to "arxivcite.org/pdf/..." to access arxivcite.

For example, if you want to cite:

https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13033

then access:

https://arxivcite.org/abs/2411.13033
